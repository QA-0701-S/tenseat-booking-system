Service

TenSeat provides booking software for restaurants. Restaurants receive a booking page, an owner dashboard, service-hour controls, capacity controls, booking codes, cancellation tools, and subscription billing features.

Restaurant Responsibility

Restaurants are responsible for the accuracy of their restaurant details, service hours, capacity settings, booking availability, guest communications, and any legal obligations connected with their restaurant operations.

Guest Bookings

A booking made through TenSeat is a request or reservation with the restaurant shown on the booking page. The restaurant remains responsible for honouring, changing, refusing, or cancelling bookings where necessary.

Trials And Subscriptions

Normal restaurant registration includes a 14-day free trial. Registration with a valid referral code includes a 30-day free trial. After the trial, restaurants must subscribe to a paid plan to continue accepting new online bookings.

Basic is A$10 per month. Pro is A$20 per month. Prices are listed in Australian dollars unless stated otherwise. Payments are processed by Stripe.

Cancellation

Restaurants can manage or cancel their subscription through the Stripe Billing Portal from the owner dashboard. Cancelling or failing to pay may pause new online bookings. Existing booking records may remain available for restaurant reference.

Referral Credits

Each paid restaurant may receive one referral code. A referral code can be shared with multiple new restaurants, but each new restaurant can use only one referral code during registration. When a referred restaurant completes its first paid month, the referring restaurant may receive one month of credit.

Referral credits are capped at 12 months per restaurant. Credits are not cash, cannot be withdrawn, and cannot be transferred to another restaurant.

Acceptable Use

Restaurants must not use TenSeat for spam, fraudulent bookings, misleading business information, unlawful activity, attempts to bypass billing, or interference with the service.

Service Availability

TenSeat aims to keep the service available, but downtime can happen because of hosting, maintenance, third-party services, internet issues, or other events. Restaurants should keep their own operational backup process for critical bookings.
