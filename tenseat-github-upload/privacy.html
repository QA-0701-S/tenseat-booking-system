Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Who This Policy Covers

This policy explains how TenSeat handles personal information for restaurant owners, restaurant staff, and guests who make bookings through a TenSeat booking page.

Information We Collect

For restaurant accounts, TenSeat collects restaurant name, login email, password hash, address, Google Maps search text, service hours, capacity settings, Stripe billing identifiers, referral codes, and account activity needed to run the service.

For guest bookings, TenSeat collects booking date, time, party size, first name, last name, phone number, optional email, optional notes, booking code, and booking status.

How We Use Information

We use this information to create restaurant booking pages, receive and manage bookings, prevent overbooking, send booking confirmation emails when configured, handle cancellations, manage subscriptions, support referral credits, secure accounts, and maintain the service.

Sharing Information

Guest booking details are shown to the restaurant that receives the booking. Payment processing is handled by Stripe. Email delivery may use Gmail SMTP or another configured provider. TenSeat does not sell guest or restaurant personal information.

Storage And Security

TenSeat stores account and booking data on the configured hosting environment. Passwords are salted and hashed. Access to restaurant booking details requires restaurant login. The service uses HTTPS in production and basic rate limits for sensitive actions.

Access, Correction, And Deletion

Restaurant owners can update restaurant details from the dashboard. To request access, correction, export, or deletion of account or booking information, contact TenSeat support using the contact details provided by the operator of this service.

Retention

TenSeat keeps booking and account records while needed to provide the service, handle billing, support restaurants, prevent fraud, and meet business or legal record-keeping needs. Records that are no longer needed should be deleted or de-identified where practical.

Changes

TenSeat may update this policy as the product changes. The updated date at the top of this page shows the current version.
